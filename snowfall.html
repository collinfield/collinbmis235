Snowfall at Mammoth Mountain

Home About Us Challenging Runs Snowfall

Mammoth Mountain is famous for its impressive snowfall. On average, the mountain receives over 400 inches of snow annually, making it a prime destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

  • Record Snowfall: Over 600 inches in a single season!
  • Average Season: December to April, with peak snowfall in January and February.
  • Snow Depth: Consistently deep powder for an excellent skiing experience.
