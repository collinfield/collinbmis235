Challenging Runs at Mammoth Mountain

Home About Us Challenging Runs

For thrill-seekers, Mammoth Mountain offers some of the most intense and exhilarating ski runs. Here are a few of the most challenging:

  • Hangman's Hollow - Known for its steep drop and narrow path, this run is for experts only!
  • Climax - Sitting at the summit, Climax offers an incredible view followed by an intense descent.
  • Paranoid Flats - With ungroomed snow and a steep incline, this is a favorite among advanced skiers.
